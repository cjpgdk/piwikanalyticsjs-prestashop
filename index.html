PiwikAnalyticsJS is a Piwik Analytics Module for PrestaShop

Keep track your visitors and analyse your shop effectiveness with Piwik Analytics in PrestaShop 1.4, 1.5, 1.6

About

PiwikAnalyticsJS is a PrestaShop module to track your visitors and analyse your online PrestaShop store effectiveness, using Piwik Analytics
this module uses only JavaScript to track your customers, the JavaScript code is build by the module and appended to your store in the footer,

How does it work

simply by creating the required segments of javascript code and append them to your shop's footer, based on the page that being visited in your shop and the settings you provided in the module admin

What's reported back to piwik
  • Logged in users (by id)
  • Page views
  • Shopping cart
  • Orders
  • Product views
  • Product id, reference and attribute ids
  • Category views
  • Purchased products
  • Total income
  • Total tax
  • Total shipping
  • And much more, all thanks to the wonders of Piwik Analytics
